Areas of Experience and Interest

The reason I choose the backend class is that I have been interested in data management and APIs since taking an online full-stack web development course.

During the course, I have got access to both frontend and backend sections, what has impressed me most is APIs in the backend section. By implementing third-party APIs to get third-party data and services, I can focus on my own unique functionality and surround it with fully functional, distributed processes developed by others. It is amazing that I am able to combine different data to create different interpretation of data and new services. On the other hand, I have also learned that the data management is an important issue, such as security and scalability, to protect data and provide good service, which is challenging but rewarding.

I have dived deeper by taking other online courses about node.js, database and APIs, practicing some projects (like the blog), watching videos on freeCodeCamp’s youtube channel, reading articles and books about those topics, etc. However, I know what I have learned is far from enough to be a professional backend engineer and I am eager to push myself further. Therefore I wish to have the opportunity to join the course to really become a professional backend engineer.
